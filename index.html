Snally

Where cryptids code, data shapeshifts, and experiments escape the lab.

Some sites wear neckties, but this one shows up in neon sneakers, half finished prototypes tucked under its arm. It is a sandbox for wild web builds and offbeat data driven sketches, a playground where folklore trades campfire stories for JavaScript, CSS, and Quarto.

Numbers here morph into playful visuals, and HTML stretches into unexpected shapes. One week you might find a data driven journey through long distance running, the next an interactive monster sighting map or a retro inspired game.

Think of it as a garage after midnight. Lights on, coffee brewing, code flickering across screens. Wander in, tap a few buttons, and discover what has hatched today.
